🎮 Games

Take a break and enjoy some browser-based games

All work and no play makes for a dull researcher. Enjoy some classic browser-based games as a break from research and deep work. These games don't require installation—just click and play!

🦖 Chrome Dinosaur Game

Easy

The classic offline dinosaur game from Google Chrome. Simple but addictive—avoid obstacles and see how high you can score. Perfect for quick breaks!

How to play: Press SPACE or UP ARROW to jump. Avoid the cacti!

🟢 Agar.io

Easy-Medium

Grow your cell by consuming others while avoiding bigger cells. Multiplayer online game where you compete with other players. Simple mechanics, engaging gameplay.

How to play: Move mouse to control. Click to split. Eat smaller cells, avoid bigger ones!

Play Game

🐍 Slither.io

Easy-Medium

Control a snake and grow by eating glowing dots. Compete with other players online. Quick reflexes and strategic positioning are key. Highly addictive!

How to play: Move mouse to control. Boost with SPACE. Eat dots, avoid other snakes!

Play Game

🔢 2048

Easy-Hard

Slide numbered tiles to combine them and reach the 2048 tile. Puzzle game that's easy to learn but challenging to master. Great for strategic thinking!

How to play: Use arrow keys to slide tiles. Combine matching numbers. Reach 2048!

Play Game

🐦 Flappy Bird

Medium

Simple but challenging—tap to make the bird jump and avoid pipes. This deceptively simple game is extremely hard to master. How many pipes can you pass?

How to play: Click or tap to jump. Avoid the pipes. Simple mechanics, brutal difficulty!

Play Game

📝 Wordle

Easy-Medium

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries. Use color feedback to narrow down possibilities. One puzzle per day or play unlimited. Great for vocabulary and logical thinking!

How to play: Type a 5-letter word. Green = correct position, Yellow = wrong position, Gray = not in word

Gaming Philosophy

Games are more than entertainment—they're interactive systems that teach problem-solving, strategy, and persistence. Whether it's puzzle games that exercise logic or action games that test reflexes, gaming is a valuable form of recreation and mental exercise.

As someone interested in interactive media and game design, I appreciate games as systems. They combine mechanics, narrative, aesthetics, and player agency into compelling experiences.
