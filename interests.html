Interests & Passions

Beyond the research: what drives curiosity and creativity

Professional Interests

🔬

Open Science

Passionate about making research accessible and reproducible. I believe in sharing datasets, code, and findings with the community. Open science accelerates progress and builds trust in research outcomes.

🌍

Election Integrity & Democracy

Technology plays a critical role in protecting democratic processes. My work on bot detection and election security reflects deep concern for authentic civic engagement and informed decision-making.

🔐

Digital Rights & Privacy

Technology should empower users while protecting their privacy. I'm interested in systems that provide security and privacy without sacrificing transparency or usability.

🌱

Sustainable Technology

ML systems have significant computational costs. I'm interested in developing efficient algorithms and exploring how technology can contribute to environmental sustainability.

🏥

Healthcare & Well-being

Applying ML to healthcare to improve outcomes and quality of care. My TSX engine demonstrates how data-driven insights can enhance clinical decision-making while maintaining explainability.

📚

Education & Mentorship

Developing practitioners who combine technical excellence with ethical awareness. Teaching is about preparing the next generation to address tomorrow's challenges responsibly.

Personal Passions

🎵

Music Production

Electronic music production as creative expression. Music combines mathematics, creativity, and emotion— much like research. Active on SoundCloud as AADI Records exploring ambient and experimental sounds.

🎨

Visual Arts & Design

Interest in visual design, typography, and digital art. Design shapes how people interact with technology. I enjoy creating aesthetically meaningful interfaces and experiences.

🎮

Games & Interactive Media

Games are fascinating systems that combine storytelling, interaction design, and complex mechanics. I enjoy exploring how games can be educational and entertaining simultaneously.

✈️

Travel & Cultural Exchange

Having lived and worked in multiple countries (Pakistan, USA) has enriched my perspective. I'm interested in understanding different cultures, perspectives, and how technology serves different communities.

🏞️

Nature & Outdoor Activities

Balance to screen time. Exploring natural environments, hiking, and appreciating landscapes provides perspective and inspiration for creative thinking.

📖

Science Fiction & Futurism

Science fiction explores possibilities and implications of technology. Interested in how emerging tech might shape society and what ethical frameworks we need.

Core Values

🎯 Excellence

Pursuit of quality in research, code, teaching, and life. Excellence is not perfection, but genuine effort to create value and minimize harm.

🤝 Integrity

Honesty in research, transparency in communication, and consistency between words and actions. Integrity builds trust and enables collaboration.

🌟 Impact

Work should matter. Whether through research, teaching, or building systems, I aim to create positive impact for individuals and communities.

🔄 Continuous Learning

Technology and knowledge evolve constantly. Commitment to lifelong learning keeps work relevant and enables adaptation to new challenges.

🌍 Responsibility

Technology has power. Using it responsibly means considering implications for privacy, security, equity, and social good.

✨ Creativity

Innovation comes from creative thinking and exploring unconventional approaches. Balancing rigor with creativity enables breakthrough ideas.
