⚖️ Setup Guide

1

Hang a ruler/stick from a string at its center point. Tape your phone to one end and a small cup to the other.

2

Let it settle until the stability bar turns green. Tap Zero Scale.

3

Place a known weight (coin) in the cup. Enter its weight above and tap Calibrate.

4

Remove the coin. Place any small item in the cup to measure it!
