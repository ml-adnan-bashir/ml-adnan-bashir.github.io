Tilt Scale

Turn your phone into a precision balance using its built-in gyroscope.

You'll need: a ruler or stick, a string, some tape, and a small cup.

🔧

Build Your Balance

1. Tie a string to the exact middle of a ruler.

2. Tape your phone flat onto one end.

3. Attach a small cup at the other end, same distance from center.

4. Hang it from a hook or doorknob so it swings freely.
