Phone Tilt Scale

Instructions:

  1. Balance the exact middle of your phone on a soft sponge.
  2. Wait for the phone to settle, then tap Zero Scale.
  3. Place a known weight (like a 5g coin) at the very top edge.
  4. Type that weight in the box below and tap Calibrate.
  5. Remove the coin. Place an unknown item at the top edge to weigh it!
